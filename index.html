Clinical Informatics

Industry leading expertise in openEHR strategy, analysis, design, development, governance and curation of openEHR clinical models.

Training and Mentoring

Specialist training and practical mentoring for individuals and organisations expanding their knowledge and expertise in clinical informatics.

Implementation support

Our team can provide support with data and terminology mapping, data exchange and technical guidance for implementations across a range of technologies.

Shape your openEHR Strategy

We offer over 30 years practical informatics experience in the field, with a solid background in front-line clinical practice and real world experience. We will help you shape your openEHR strategy and provide practical advice and guidance.

Practical Design, Development, Training and Governance

Our team can support you to design, develop and govern your clinical models, either with a fully managed service or through a skills transfer approach. We provide a wide range of approaches to suit your needs, including online training, face to face and mentoring approaches.

Navigate your Implementations

Our team will support your implementations of standards/platfrom-based systems. We have extensive expertise in detailed clinical information modelling using archetype-based methodologies such as openEHR, in conjunction with clinical terminologies such as SNOMED CT, dm+d and ICDxx, and data exchange standards such as HL7 V2, CDA and HL7 FHIR.
